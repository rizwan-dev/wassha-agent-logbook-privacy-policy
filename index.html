Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026

Wassha Agent Logbook (the "App") is developed and operated by Wassha Inc. ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, share, and protect information when you use the App. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: name, agent ID, phone number, and login credentials used to access the App.
  • Activity and logbook data: records entered by agents, including sales, stock, transaction details, timestamps, and notes related to daily agent operations.
  • Location data: approximate or precise location, where required to verify agent visits or activity location.
  • Device information: device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and app version.
  • Usage data: log data such as access times, app crashes, and feature usage, collected automatically to help us maintain and improve the App.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the App's core functionality (agent logbook management).
  • To verify agent identity and activity for business reporting purposes.
  • To monitor, analyze, and improve the performance and reliability of the App.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or misuse.
  • To communicate with users about updates, support, or operational matters.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With Wassha group companies and affiliates for business operations and reporting.
  • With service providers and contractors who perform services on our behalf (e.g. cloud hosting, analytics), under confidentiality obligations.
  • If required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Wassha, our users, or others.

4. Data Storage and Security

Information collected through the App is stored on secure servers. We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal and activity data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, including for record-keeping, business reporting, and legal compliance. When data is no longer needed, it is deleted or anonymized.

6. Permissions Used by the App

The App may request the following device permissions, which are used solely to provide its core features:

  • Location: to record where agent activities take place.
  • Storage/Camera: to attach photos or files to logbook entries, if applicable.
  • Network access: to sync logbook data with our servers.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by authorized agents and business personnel and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
